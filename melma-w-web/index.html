Medical Research Disclaimer

The MELMA-W Evaluator is a research tool designed for the benchmarking and clinical evaluation of Large Language Models (LLMs). The scores and rationales generated by the integrated AI reviewer are based on the MELMA-CAF (Clinical Acceptability Framework) and do not constitute professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Evaluations are performed by an automated system and may contain inaccuracies. This tool is intended for use by qualified researchers and healthcare professionals as part of structured AI validation studies. Never disregard professional medical advice or delay seeking it because of information processed through this tool.
